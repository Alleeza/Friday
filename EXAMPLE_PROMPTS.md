# Example Game Prompts

Test prompts to verify the AI planner generates playable games with proper interactions.

## Collection Games

- Make a game where a bunny explores a forest of trees and finds carrots.
- A bunny runs around collecting coins and getting points.
- Create a game where you gather all the stars before time runs out.
- My bunny needs to find hidden gifts in a garden full of flowers.
- Collect all the gems while avoiding the rocks.

## Exploration Games

- A bunny wanders through a forest and discovers surprises.
- Make a world where a bunny explores and meets different animals.
- Create an adventure where you roam around and find secret things.
- Build a garden world where a bunny walks around and finds mushrooms.

## Maze / Navigation Games

- Guide a bunny through a maze of rocks to reach the goal flag.
- Navigate through obstacles to get to the finish line.
- Create a path through trees and rocks that leads to a key.
- Build a maze where the bunny has to reach the castle.

## Dodge / Survival Games

- Dodge the rocks while trying to survive as long as possible.
- A bunny has to avoid falling rocks in a cave.
- Survive the asteroid field by dodging space rocks.
- Stay alive while frogs hop around and try to bump into you.

## Mixed / Creative Games

- A bunny collects carrots in a forest while avoiding rocks.
- Race through a field of trees to reach the goal and collect coins along the way.
- Explore a spooky world with ghosts and find hidden hearts.
- A robot collects keys to unlock gifts while moving through a field of rocks.

## Edge Cases (Infeasible Ideas)

These should trigger the infeasibility protocol but still produce an adapted, playable plan:

- Make a shooter game where the bunny fires lasers at enemies.
- Create a platformer where the bunny jumps over gaps.
- Build a multiplayer game where two bunnies race each other.
- Make a game with health bars and damage when you hit enemies.
